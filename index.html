The 5-star Gran La Florida GL Monumento is located on Tibidabo Hill and offers views of Barcelona and the Mediterranean Sea. Guests have free access to the spa. Some rooms also have a private balcony. The Gran La Florida GL Monumento has panoramic views from its gardens and terraces. The L'Occitane Spa features indoor and outdoor stainless steel pools.
